Design of Weight and Height Measurement System Based Wireless Communication

Paper Published by Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)

This project is built to help the hospital system with the height and weight measurement of patients during the pandemic. It's combined with IoT and telecommunication technology complete with high-end quality sensors that are used for detecting the user. The system also improved with mathematics algorithm, therefore the features could be developed by displaying BMI(Body Mass Index) Value. The tools provide real-time data measurement which can monitor through the website for the medical staff as well an LCD screen can access the user of tools.
